FieldReport Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

COLOR: Burnt Orange #CC5500

Privacy First: FieldReport is designed with your privacy as the top priority. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, share, or sell any of your information.

1. Information We Collect

FieldReport stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Report Content: Names, locations, notes, and observations you enter
  • Photos: Images you attach to reports
  • User Preferences: App settings and customizations
  • Templates: Custom report templates you create

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your reports within the app
  • Generate PDF exports when requested
  • Provide search and filtering functionality
  • Remember your preferences and settings

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored on your device using encrypted SQLite database (AES-256 encryption)
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not upload your data to any servers
  • No Backups to Our Servers: Your device backups are controlled by Android, not by us

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Period.

5. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data.
